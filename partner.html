Become a Covenant Partner

As a Covenant Partner you are recognized as an integral part of the ministry, and you are highly valued for what you make possible for others.

Prophetess Victoria travels the world ministering to hundreds of needy souls and you partake with her the blessing she receives due to this work. Through this website, we trust God for more impact as people share their prayer items and testimonies.

Through one on one coaching, teaching, local & international outreach, Victoria Michaels Prophetic Voice Ministry gives our Partners the opportunity to fulfill Matthew 28: 19 “Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you.” from where they are. Your financial support and prayers enables us to proclaim the good news of Jesus Christ, transform lives, heal hearts, and win souls.

We are daily praying for you, your loved ones, and your needs, that God may raise you up to His level of expectation and fulfillment.
